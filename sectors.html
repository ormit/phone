Engineering Sector

ngineering recruitment is the core of our business, in the 5 years since we started M65 Recruitment have been and are involved with some of East Lancs leading Engineering companies. Manufacturing Sector

We tend to find on the manufacturing side the vast majority of work is either ongoing temp or temp to perm positions. M65 Recruitment work closely with companies going to great lengths to understand their needs and respond quickly to the demands that a busy temp desk can generate. Over time we have built sound working partnerships with clients, really getting to know their business, supplying to some leading players in the manufacturing sector for the area, the requirements can vary a lot from Assemblers, to pickers and packers to FLT drivers. The consultant has a number of years experience within this sector and will always ensure that clients requirements are met.


Commercial Sector

The Commercial sector has grown on the back of the success of the Engineering and the Manufacturing Sectors, our key clients will often approach M65 Recruitment for their commercial vacancies. On the Commercial side we will often get permanent vacancies as well as temp or maternity cover vacancies. We really do cover a broad range of Commercial vacancies such as Purchasing, Sales, Accounts, Admin and Customer Service, all in and around the M65 corridor. Our consultant is very personable and builds rapport with clients and is always on hand to answer any questions you may have.
